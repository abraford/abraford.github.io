Dodge & Burn re-creation

This was my final project of Web Development 1 at the Minneapolis College of Art and Design. I had wanted to do a re-creation of a much easier site, but a friend of mine recommended this one, from a boutique shop in Brooklyn.

The sprites were a challenge, took me forever to figure out. I decided to build the display section as a list instead of a set of divs. Not sure if that was the bet way - the original site uses divs - but it was what I went with. I never could get the bottom form to line up correctly.
