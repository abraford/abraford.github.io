The Wreck of the Old 97

Like a lot of people, I like to listen to music when I'm doing school work. Mostly, I listen to classical music stations over the internet - KUSC out of Los Angeles is my favorite - but on this night, I was listening to bluegrass out of Virginia, WNRV AM 990.

I was pretty desperate to come up with a decent subject for a responsive website. Sometime around 3 a.m., a version of the old folk train song "The Wreck of the Old 97."

I knew the song from the Johnny Cash version, but other than that, I knew nothing of the song or the wreck. A quick look to Wikipedia and I was on my way.

This is my most succesful marriage of design and responsiveness. The drop down links are as close to interactivity as I've gotten.
