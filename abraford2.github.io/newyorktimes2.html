Re-creation of a New York Times page, from April 16, 2014

This is a page that did just for fun, between semesters. I especially wanted to challenge myself to build a graphic using HTML and CSS, instead of just placing a pdf on a page. I built this from the top down, trying to pick apart and reassemble little bits of the original page. I was gratified - and relieved - that this was met with good reviews from the developers at the Times. As with all these projects, what I learned is that I still have a lot to learn.
