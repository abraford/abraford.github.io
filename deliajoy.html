Nearly a year

This is my first multipage website. I couldn't have been more pleased that it actually worked! It has links and galleries! It passed the validator! My wife, a graphic designer, told me that this would have been an awesome site in 1993.

But, this is where I started. I wanted to push myself from the beginning to do the best work that I could.

So today, if I was building this again, I would do it a lot differently. Actual galleries, and an improved design come to mind. But this is the student work that I'll always look back on with a lot of fondness.
